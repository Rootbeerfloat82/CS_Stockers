# CS_Stockers
Experience the thrill of the stock market in real-time with our cutting-edge Flask Python website. Dive into the world of finance with our interactive stock simulator powered by live API data, providing an authentic and dynamic trading environment. Stay engaged and informed as our website features two AI chat assistants, offering valuable insights, answering queries, and guiding you through your investment journey. Witness your progress come to life with automatically updating charts that reflect real-time stock values, allowing you to track your portfolio's performance seamlessly. Whether you're a seasoned investor or a curious beginner, our Flask website offers a user-friendly and immersive experience, combining the excitement of a live stock market with the convenience of AI-powered assistance and visually appealing, dynamically updating charts.
